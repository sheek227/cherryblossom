Cherry Blossom

Simone Tripoli

My style is called Cherry Blossom and it is based on the artwork of Hiroshi Yoshida.

I drew the inspiration from my favorite paintings of Hiroshi Yoshida; specifically, the painting of the cherry blossom tree.

In this theme, sakura/cherry blossom flowers represent capital cities. Other cities are represented with the regular dot. This is because cherry blossoms only bloom for one month throughout the year, so I liked the idea of making this icon rare.

For the road network, the major roads are colored in a dark brown. This is to create the illusion that the roads are branches. Meanwhile, the shields are pretty standard. I made the font of the numbers the same as the font of the city/country/state names: Knewave Regular.

The map loses texture as you zoom in.
